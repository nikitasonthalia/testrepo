About Us

Sharing on Wheels is our attempt to make the commute cleaner,greener and simpler by promoting car pooling amongst ITU community.Sharing on wheels is website for ITU students,faculties and staff members to choose a car pool among their fellow mates and get along while communiting to and fro the campus.We offer people to register their trips as Driver and Riders, edit their trips if plan changes or search for trips in a variety of ways.This way there will be less cars on the road, plus the ITU staff,faculties and students will get a good chance to knoweach other and make friends.
